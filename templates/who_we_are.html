OUR WHY

We noticed the industry is not set up to service brands as they currently exist in the world. Teams are inherently siloed, which leads to miscommunication and marketing inefficiencies. Because brands are built towards efficiency, there’s a general lack of common language within the marketing ecosystem, poor cross functional communication, and a misunderstanding of culture.

At the Right Hand, we’re building a new kind of agency around people who care about results, not awards.

Our work is rooted in our principles.

COLLABORATION

It all begins and ends with collaboration. We onboard like a new hire and act as an extension of your team, not an outside partner. We believe the best work doesn't feel transactional.

CONVERSATION

We take the "don't talk at your consumers, talk to them" approach with our work. We prefer conversations over presentations. Our work isn't about showing off how smart we are, it's about solving your problems effectively and efficiently.

CREATIVITY

We understand there are more than one way to solve challenges. We use our creative problem solving and original thinking to make your brand stand out and your dollars work harder.
